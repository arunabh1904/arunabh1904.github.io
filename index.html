Hello, I'm Arunabh, and welcome to my personal space.

I currently work in computer vision research at Zoox, developing models to interpret driving scenes. I also co-organize the perception research group at Zoox, where we explore state-of-the-art research and bring promising ideas into production. Previously, I led part of the perception team at DEKA, focusing on short-range perception and traffic light detection for Roxo, FedEx's last-mile delivery robot.

My robotics journey began in India, where I earned my undergraduate degree from MIT, Manipal. Although I discovered robotics somewhat by chance, choosing to focus on autonomous driving technology was intentional—I believe in its potential to save lives. This conviction brought me to the U.S. nearly a decade ago for graduate studies at the Colorado School of Mines.

I'm driven to keep building, keep learning, and—like a shooting star blazing across the sky—hopefully leave my own mark on the world!
